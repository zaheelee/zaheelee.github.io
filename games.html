Resident Medieval

Over the course of 6 weeks, my team of 10 Programmers and 4 Psychology students were tasked with creating a game that would use types of exposure therapy to assist players with overcoming their real world social anxiety. It was the first time any of the programmers had worked on a game, and the first time the Psychology students had written a gameplay script.

Resident Medieval represents an amazing learning experience for every Programmer involved. Almost everyone on my team went on to take the Advanced Game Design course offered at CWRU, where their skills improved along with the quality of their games. I also learned a lot from this game, as it taught me how to manage a team of Game Designers, Gameplay Programmers, and Writers.
